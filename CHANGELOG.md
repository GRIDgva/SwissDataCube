- maxBounds to restrict the view on Switzerland
- Zoom to default extent

- About section

- Help section
- Mouse Position
- Scale Bar
- Webservices; relevant links for Discovery (CSW, OpenSearch EO); View (WMS, WMTS, TMS, WMS-C); Download (WCS, WFS) services
